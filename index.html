About Me

Hello, my name is Andres Garcia Gamino. I am a senior at Canoga Park High School and I am finishing my second year at the Northrup Grumman Program, HIP, High School Involvement Program. I would like to pursue a career in mechanical engineering. I want a career in mechanical engineering because I enjoy thinking outside the box to solve difficult problems or to find a better solution to a problem that has already been solved. Since I was a child, I had always displayed an interest in mathematics and science, but I never knew if I wanted to pursue a career relating to these areas. I took many difficult math classes and science classes to see if my interests would persist, and fortunately, they have. I strive to fullfill both of these desires by choosing a career that has both aspects, which is where engineering comes in. I decided to apply to the HIP Program to see how I could nuture these interests or to see if I liked engineering in general, and so far, the journey has been great.
